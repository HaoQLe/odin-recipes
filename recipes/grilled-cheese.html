Grilled Cheese

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a simple yet delicious dish made with just a few ingredients, featuring melted cheese sandwiched between two crispy, buttery slices of bread. It's a comfort food staple beloved by many for its gooey center and golden-brown exterior. This recipe serves one but can easily be multiplied for more servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (e.g., Cheddar, American, Swiss)
  • 1-2 tablespoons butter, room temperature
  • Optional: Pinch of salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare Sandwich: Place cheese between two bread slices. Optionally, add salt and pepper.
  2. Butter Bread: Butter the outside of each bread slice.
  3. Cook: Preheat a skillet over medium heat. Grill sandwich for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and cheese melts.
  4. Serve: Let sit for a minute, then cut and serve warm.
