Matcha Latte

Description

A Matcha Latte is a smooth, creamy beverage that combines the earthy flavors of matcha green tea powder with the richness of milk, sweetened to taste. It's a popular choice for those seeking a healthier alternative to coffee, offering a gentle caffeine boost along with antioxidants. This recipe is for a single serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon matcha green tea powder
  • 2 tablespoons hot water (not boiling, about 175°F or 80°C)
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or any plant-based milk like almond, soy, or oat)
  • 1-2 teaspoons honey or sugar (optional, adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes (for iced matcha latte, optional)

Steps

  1. Sift the Matcha: Begin by sifting your matcha green tea powder into a cup or bowl to remove any clumps. This ensures a smooth latte.
  2. Whisk with Hot Water: Add the hot water to the sifted matcha. Using a bamboo whisk (chasen) or a small regular whisk, vigorously whisk in a zigzag motion until the tea is frothy. This step is crucial for creating the perfect texture and bringing out the flavors of the matcha.
  3. Heat the Milk: Warm your milk in a saucepan over medium heat until it's hot but not boiling, or use a milk steamer if you have one. For an iced latte, skip this step.
  4. Froth the Milk: If you like your latte frothy, use a milk frother to aerate the milk until it's light and foamy. This step can be skipped for a simpler version.
  5. Combine and Sweeten: Pour the matcha mixture into your serving cup. Add the heated or frothed milk on top. Stir in honey or sugar if desired, adjusting the sweetness to your taste.
  6. Serve: For a hot latte, enjoy it immediately. For an iced version, add ice cubes to your serving cup before adding the matcha and frothed milk.
