Omelette

Description

This quick and easy breakfast omelette recipe is perfect for a cozy morning. It combines fluffy, seasoned eggs with your choice of fresh fillings like cheese, veggies, or ham, all folded into a warm, delicious omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1-2 tbsp milk or water (optional, for fluffiness)
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Your choice of fillings (e.g., shredded cheese, diced ham, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, spinach)

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and pepper, and whisk until well combined. Add a splash of milk or water for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add butter or oil and let it melt, coating the pan evenly.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan, swirling it gently to spread it evenly across the bottom.
  4. Once the edges start to set but the middle is still a bit runny, add your desired fillings on one half of the omelette.
  5. Carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the eggs are fully set, then slide the omelette onto a plate.
