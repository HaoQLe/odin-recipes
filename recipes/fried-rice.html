Fried Rice

Description

This fried rice recipe is a quick and flavorful way to use up leftover rice, combining tender vegetables and savory seasonings into a satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1-2 tbsp oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Â½ cup mixed vegetables (like peas, carrots, and corn)
  • 1-2 tbsp soy sauce (or to taste)

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the oil and let it warm up.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Then, add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  3. Break up the rice with your hands if clumped, then add it to the pan. Stir-fry with the vegetables for a couple of minutes to heat it through.
  4. Add the soy sauce and any additional ingredients like scrambled egg or cooked chicken. Stir well, ensuring the rice is evenly coated.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped green onions and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade fried rice!
